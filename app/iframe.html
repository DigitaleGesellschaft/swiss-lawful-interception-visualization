How to integrate the visualization

You can integrate the visualization into your webiste. Therefore you need to use an iFrame. Use the HTML-Snippet below to integrate it. It is possible to adapt the size of the iFrame as well as the language, year or canton to load. For example if you want to load directly the german version of Zurich in 2013 you can adapt the path into 

https://www.digitale-gesellschaft.ch/sliv/de/2013/zh
